Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

SubScan ("we", "our", or "us") provides this Privacy Policy to inform you of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information from users of the SubScan mobile application ("App") for Android. This App is designed as a local-first utility for subscription management.

2. Information We Access

SubScan is built on privacy-first principles. We only access the following information on your device:

  • SMS Messages: The App reads financial SMS messages locally on the user's device to detect recurring subscription payments such as Netflix, Spotify, utilities, or other digital services.
  • Local Subscription Data: Based on the SMS analysis, the App generates and stores information regarding your detected subscriptions, such as service names, transaction amounts, and renewal dates.

3. How Data Is Used

The information accessed by SubScan is used exclusively to:

  • Automatically detect and track recurring subscription payments from your SMS inbox.
  • Provide you with an on-device dashboard for managing and analyzing your subscription spending.
  • Generate local notifications for upcoming subscription renewals.

Strict Local Processing: All analysis of your SMS messages and financial data happens locally on your device. SubScan does not perform any cloud-based processing, nor does it support or perform analysis of PDF bank statements, CSV uploads, or browser-based transaction tracking. No SMS messages or personal data are uploaded to external servers.

4. Data Storage

All detected subscription data is stored locally on the user's device using the App's internal secure database. We do not provide any cloud synchronization or remote backup services for your data.

5. Data Sharing

SubScan does not sell, share, or transmit user SMS data or personal information to third parties. Your data remains entirely on your device and is not shared with any analytics providers, marketing firms, or external service providers.

6. Permissions Used

To provide its core functionality, SubScan requires the following Android permissions:

  • READ_SMS: Required to scan your message history for existing subscription-related transactions.
  • RECEIVE_SMS: Required to detect and notify you of new subscription payments in real-time.
  • INTERNET: Required for standard application lifecycle functions and updates; however, this permission is not used to transmit your SMS or financial data to any external server.

The App identifies financial SMS messages locally on the device to detect recurring payments. No SMS data is transmitted over the network.

7. Children's Privacy

SubScan is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children under the age of 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information on their device, please use the App's "Reset Data" feature or contact us to ensure the data is promptly removed.

8. Changes to Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy within the App's settings screen. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically for any updates.
